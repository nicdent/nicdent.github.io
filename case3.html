Realestate.com.au is the Australian leader in residential property experiences. It is a part of the REA Group - a world leading property network with brands and offices throughout Australia, Asia, and North America.

Role

I was a Senior UX / UI Designer working on the new property management product for realestate.com.au customers. I was fully remote for this role being based in Sydney with the rest of the team based at the Melbourne head office.

The design team was embedded within cross-functional agile teams that support different products and platforms. The team utilised an adapted user-centred design process that aligned with the overall business rhythm.

The wider REA Group design team of 40+ designers and researchers worked together developing process efficiencies and design systems.

Projects

The projects I completed were part of the first launch of the new property management product Ignite. Ignite was built for property professionals to help manage their listings on realestate.com.au.

My responsibilities during projects:

  • User research & testing
  • Workshops & presentations
  • IA, journeys & task flows
  • Wireframing, interaction design & UI design
  • Low to high fidelity prototyping
  • User analytics analysis
