Online invoicing

Giving small businesses more control of their cash flow and getting them paid faster.

Project overview

MYOB is the market leader for business management solutions in Australia & New Zealand. The company makes a range of desktop, web and mobile products used by over 1 million customers.

Invoicing is an important part of every business, itâ€™s what gets you paid. Businesses want to spend less time chasing payments.

MYOB products are integral to businesses invoicing needs but a pure online invoicing service was a missing part of the MYOB experiences. The goal was to create a service that could be integrated with several existing MYOB accounting products to help businesses get paid faster.

The project team was an agile scrum team, with a Product Owner, Lead Developer, Infrastructure Architect, QA, Iteration Manager and me the UX Designer. Also extensive collaboration with several other product teams at MYOB that would use the new service to deliver invoices from their products.

Discover & Identify

Working with existing applications allowed the team to discover how the invoicing process currently works.

I led the following research -

  • Informal interviews with existing MYOB users to build empathy and identify recurring pain points.
  • Interviews with support staff.
  • Interviews with stakeholders from applications which had invoicing functions.
  • Competitor analysis.

The team came together at inception to make our initial assumptions.

  • I illustrated the current user flows and pain points.
  • I used audio clips of user interviews to share empathy for users with the team.

Multiple hypotheses were formed, these were prioritised according to the effort required to validate and value added.

Fast validation

We created a register your interest for online invoicing. The assumed most needed features of the product got marketed to a segment of users. Users needed to connect their accounts via a signup page to register interest. Connecting actual accounts showed a real commitment and want for the features.

Create & Build

As we were working to lean startup principles, only the lightest weight deliverable was created to validate each hypothesis.

I was responsible for creating various testable and releasable deliverables including -

  • Wireframes
  • Prototypes - Invision, HTML & CSS
  • Finished UI designs
  • Deployable HTML & CSS

Designs incorporated -

  • Current best design practices
  • Qualitative and quantitative data from experiments
  • Company style guides
  • Collaboration with other designers and team members.

Deliverables were kept as live documentation as much as possible to keep the many teams on the same page at all times.

Tools used - Sketching, Balsamiq, LucidChart, Photoshop, InVision, Sublime Text.

Building a BETA lab

A BETA product using APIs to send invoices from a major accounting app became the initial MVP. The build time was much faster by not disrupting the existing product. Experiments with real users and monitoring real invoices sent with the BETA service unlocked great quantitative data.

Experiment & Learn

Through experimentation we validated and refined ideas before going too far down any path.

I managed and facilitated the user testing sessions that ran on a fortnightly basis. The sessions included testing prototypes and interviewing users on site and remotely.

All sessions were open to all team members to attend utilising the on-site focus room if needed. The sessions were recorded and key results shared with documented quotes or video clips.

Tools used - Camtasia, gotomeeting, InVision, Google Analytics, Intercom.

BETA experiments

Experiments in BETA were continually filtered through to existing MYOB products once proven to be beneficial to users.

Project outcome

The invoicing service is now fully integrated with 2 major products - MYOB AccountRight and MYOB Essentials. The invoicing service now allows MYOB users to send invoices anywhere and at anytime. It also allows their customers to pay them straight away through the online payment feature.
