Commercial Property Experiences

  • ROLE

    Senior UX / UI Designer

  • DATE

    2016-18

  • COMPANY

    realcommercial.com.au - REA Group

Realcommercial.com.au is Australia's leader in commercial property experiences. Realcommercial delivers web and native app experiences to support their user's property journeys.

Realcommercial is apart of the REA Group a leader in digital property experiences. REA has leading brands and offices throughout Australia, Asia, and the USA.

Role

I was the Senior UX / UI Designer during a large redesign of the realcommercial experiences. The redesign was planned in conjunction with multiple upgrades to technology. These dual goals meant for an iterative overhaul of all parts of the realcommercial experience.

The realcommercial design team consisted of a responsive web designer and a native app designer. Designers are imbedded within cross-functional agile teams for projects, at times designers are imbedded within multiple teams. The realcommercial design team utilised an adapted double diamond design process that aligned with the overall business rhythm.

The wider REA Group design team of 40+ designers and researchers worked together developing process efficiencies and design systems.

Projects

I was responsible for responsive web projects but at times paired with the native app designer on larger native app projects. All projects across web and native apps required designer collaboration to deliver holistic experiences for users.

Here is a selection of projects where I was the lead designer:

Property Search

Search is the foundation of a user's property journey on realcommercial.com.au.

I was responsible for the responsive web experience. I collaborated with the native app designer on the iOS and Android app redesign.

Property Search Results

Search results allow users to compare, discard and discoveer commercial properties that match their needs.

This project is the foundation of the realcommercial experience redesign. An atomic design system was put together for use on future projects. I was responsible for the responsive web experience redesign.

Property Details

Property details allow for an in-depth look into a potential property and the first point of connecting with an agent.

I was responsible for the responsive web experience redesign.

Property Invest

Realcommercial Invest is an Australian first tailored experience for commercial property investors. The experience combines unique investment data points and user-defined yield expectations within an easily comparable and sortable UI.

I was responsible for the responsive web experience redesign.
